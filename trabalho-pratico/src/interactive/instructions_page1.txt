Welcome to the interactive mode of our AirLine Query Solver! 
This feature is designed to provide a user-friendly 
environment for anyone utilizing our software, specifically 
tailored for queries related to airport data.

The primary objective of this service is to analyze and draw 
conclusions from specific types of queries based on provided 
datasets. Users can input the path to the dataset, allowing 
the system to generate insightful results.
